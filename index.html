What we
offer

Practical, engaging financial learning

We combine short video lessons, gamified modules, virtual wallets, and parent-child toolkits to help teens practice real-life money decisions. Designed for use at home, school or on simple phones.

Video Modules

Bite-sized interactive lessons on saving, budgeting and investing — accessible anytime.

Gamified Learning

Challenges, quizzes and money simulations where learners earn points, badges and recognition.

Dummy Wallet & Wallet Cashbacks

A virtual wallet for safe practice and cashback incentives for completing challenges to keep learners motivated.

Parent-Child Toolkits

Modules and conversation starters to spark healthy money talk at home and bridge generational gaps.

Gender inclusion: our priority

Targeted support to increase female participation

We proactively design incentives and activities to encourage girls' enrollment and sustained engagement.

Cashbacks for First 1000 Female Sign-ups

Early wallet cashback incentives to drive adoption and visibility among young women.

Dedicated Workshops

Exclusive in-person workshops to build confidence, address unique challenges and promote equal participation.

Parent-Child Modules

Interactive modules for parents and daughters to learn together and create supportive home environments.

Going beyond the screens

We blend digital tools with community outreach so financial learning reaches every kid — even where internet access is limited.

Offline School & Community Workshops

In-person interactive sessions in schools and residential complexes with a focus on female participation.

Inclusive Learning via SMS & WhatsApp

Short quizzes, reminders and budgeting nudges delivered directly to students' phones to bridge the digital divide.

Community Financial Literacy Fairs

Local fairs where students showcase learning through games, quizzes and mock stalls — turning education into community fun.
